The Problem

An estimated 320,000 people die due to drowning related incidents every year. Many of these deaths occus in developing countires, where SAR vessels are not present. We aim to design an inexpensive lifeboat which can easily be shipped to developing regions of the world where they are needed.

Weight

Conventional lifeboats are heavy, making them difficult to ship

Costs

Current solutions are expensive, averaging $50,000

Sustainability

Current designs are not built with sustainability in mind

Quality

Our design will follow international standards for rescue craft

Distribution

A compact portable palletable design.

Our design is compact, fitting into just 2 standard EUR/EPAL-pallets. This allows for easy storage in warehouses, with the ability of the pallets to be shipped quickly and easily at short notice.
